Why Maui?

Maui No Ka Oi (Maui is the Best)

Maui is known for its stunning natural diversity (beaches, volcanoes, rainforests), iconic drives like the Road to Hana, abundant outdoor adventures (hiking, snorkeling, whale watching), rich Hawaiian culture (luaus, history), and a perfect blend of relaxation and excitement, making it a quintessential tropical escape with something for everyone, from luxury seekers to nature lovers.

Cafes

Recommended cafes on Maui

Lava Java Coffee

Lava Java Coffee in Maui-grown coffee. They have a full coffee bar, and excellent frosty granitas. They have bags of coffee to take home, and fun items to shop and take home.

Address:

1941 S Kihei Rd, Kihei, HI

Why it's recommended

Lava Java is considered a top spot in Maui due to their focus on high-quality, locally-grown Maui coffee. They offer authentic island flavors, excellent espresso, and unique local roasts like Maui Peaberry combined with a genuine, friendly "aloha" service.

Learn More

Sugar Beach Bake Shop

Sugar Beach Bake Shop known for its freshly baked goods like popular malasadas, fresh-picked lilikoi, Dole bananas, and OMA coffee, offers a taste of local flavors in a cute setting with outdoor seating

Address:

61 S Kihei Rd, Kihei, HI

Why it's recommended

Sugar Beach Bake Shop in Kihei, Maui, is popular for its fresh, locally-inspired treats like lilikoi (passionfruit) malasadas, delicious banana bread, savory breakfast quiches, and great coffee/lemonades, using fresh Maui ingredients for authentic island flavors, making it a must-try spot for both locals and tourists seeking quality baked goods.
